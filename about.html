Sounds Merry is a record label and multimedia publisher focusing on the creative freedom of composers and artists. Sounds Merry is a place where our artists can release art that might be classified as challenging by other labels and publishers. We believe in working for the love of art first, with old-school work of mouth marketing. Sounds Merry is associated with the performance-rights organization ASCAP, based in the United States, and open to creative artists worldwide.

PUBLISHING INTERESTS:

Music: Experimental, Avant-Garde, Classical, World, Cross-Genre
Experimental Film
Interactive Art Video Games & Software
Poetry & Art Books
Fine Art Prints & Merchandise

SUBMISSION POLICIES:

Sounds Merry acts as an asset to publishing, adding an additional venue for sales and the promotion of fine art.
What We Consider: Original Music, Fine Art Prints, Poetry, Film, Art Softwares, & Re-releases of past original works (as permitted)
What We Do NOT Consider: We do not accept remixed, illegally sampled, or appropriated works. We do not accept covers where you have not obtained the necessary licenses. We do not accept re-releases of past works that are under label constraints.

If you would like to be considered, please send us your EPK to soundsmerry (at) gmail (dot) com as a link (all emails with attachments will not be read and automatically deleted). Please make sure to title "Attn: [your band or artist name] Submission" in the Subject field.
Sounds Merry is unable to respond to all submissions.

LICENSING:

If you are interested in licensing any Sounds Merry works please download and fill out the Licensing Request Form and follow the submission guidelines contained therein.

At the discretion of Sounds Merry and our artists, we may issue a license for collaborative projects that serve mutual interests. Please fill out the Licensing Request Form with extensive information about your project and include a cover letter detailing your project and medium.
Depending on the project, Sounds Merry reserves the right to deny any licencing requests.
